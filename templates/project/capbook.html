Capbook

(Python, Firebase, Roboflow, EasyOCR)


The Capbook website lets users save books by uploading pictures of their covers. It uses Artificial Intelligence to find the location of the book title by training a model with Roboflow. After finding the title's position, EasyOCR reads the book title, and the information is saved in Firebase.


Responsible for connecting the database to the front-end and training the model on Roboflow.
